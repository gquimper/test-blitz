Logotype

Product/service

The product and service logos should only be used in rare instances. Preference is to use the appropriate company logo along with the tagline. Please contact the brand team to get approval to use a product or service logo.

All product/service logos shown here and in the following page include horizontal versions for use where vertical space is at a premium.

Minimum clear space

For optimal visibility and impact, an area free of other graphics must surround product/service logos.

Minimum width

To ensure legibility, they should not appear smaller than specified.

Colour versions

The full-colour positive versions should be used whenever possible. Apart from the one-colour black version, they can also be reversed on a Bell blue or black background.
