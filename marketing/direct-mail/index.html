Direct mail is a one-on-one communication which provides an excellent opportunity to show how Bell products and services can make your life better.

1

Bell logo

Use Bell logo – preferably with tagline – at the bottom right corner of the piece.

Read more about Bell logo.

2

Bellement

Aim for a lyrical Bellement which makes the connection with the message.

3

Photography

Bright, close-cut photography on predominantly white backgrounds are the hallmark of our images.

4

Headline

It should first be clear, if possible clever, concise and benefit driven.

5

Subhead

If required, a brief description communicating a product benefit or offer.

6

Body copy

Communicate simply and with the right tone and manner

7

Graphics

See details about Graphic treatment.

8

Price point

See details about Price point treatment.

9

Call-to-action

Actionable information, typically a phone number or website address.

10

Footer

Legal disclaimers and/or footnotes from content. Also a position for partner logos.

11

Mailing info

Includes address blocks and postage indicia. Ensure proper Canada Post guidelines are followed.
