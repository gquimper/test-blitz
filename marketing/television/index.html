Marketing communications

Television

Television is the medium where our brand comes to life.

Based in the real world our TV spots should always have a positive story where the product and services are the stars.

The end conclusion of your spot should always be that thanks to Bell’s products and services, X just got better.

Bellements are to be sparsely integrated into the décor. Without being big and bold we should recognize them and they should help the viewer with brand recognition.
