Carousels

  • Full width carousels
  • Carousels cards

Carousels are used on landing pages and on the homepage to display different offers or marketing messages

  • Minimum outside padding 45 px
  • Inside padding of 15 px between elements
  • Shadow color = #000000
  • Shadow opacity = Alpha 20

Full width - Desktop

Pagination and progress indicators are always positionned at the right side of the screen.

Full width - Tablet

Pagination and progressive indicator can be positionned either at the right-hand of the screen, if the hero banner is left aligned, and each side, if the layout of the hero banner is center aligned.

Full width - Mobile

Pagination is at the left side of the screen and the progress indicator is positioned to the right of the screen.

Large desktop

We recommend that users should be able to reach the last item in the carousel in 3–4 steps (i.e., clicks or swipes).

  • Minimum outside padding 45 px
  • Inside padding of 15 px between elements
  • Shadow color = #000000
  • Shadow opacity = Alpha 20

Specific case

Under 1330px viewport, the arrow keeps a shadow.

Small desktop

We recommend that users should be able to reach the last item in the carousel in 3–4 steps (i.e., clicks or swipes).

Specific case

If the number of cards is not a multiple of 3.

Tablet

Use the illusion of continuity as a carousel cue and make sure your carousel supports swipe.

Specific case

If the number of cards is not a multiple of 2.

Mobile

Use the illusion of continuity as a carousel cue and make sure your carousel supports swipe.
