Offers

  • Primary offers
  • Secondary offers
  • Price layout

Primary offers are used mainly for Internet and TV offers on Bell.ca

  • Minimum margin of XX px
  • Inside padding of 15 px between elements
  • Surtitle 21 charaters
  • Title 42 characters
  • Background = vignette gradient

Classic offer in a container

Offer’s height is relative to content length. Full-width. Background use vignette box gradient.

Variation offer in a container

Proposition of a 3-column layout to display offer information.

Variation offer in a container with type of offer tag

Addition of the type of offer tag at the top of the box. Proposition of a 4-column layout in case of more content to display.

Variation offer in a container with logo

Proposition of a 3-column layout to display offer information. Addition of a channel logo with text content.

Variation offer in a container bonus tag

Proposition of a 3-column layout to display offer information. Addition of a Bonus tag with text content.

Secondary offer’s height is fixed to 300px on Desktop, and 445px on Tablet, where we have 2 tiles side-by-side. On mobile (767px and below), offers are stacked, so the height is relative to content.

Layout 1

This offer is composed with a title and a subtext.

When image is wide – Consider using a 50-50% image-text distribution

Layout 2

This offer is composed with a title, a subtext and legal content.

Layout 3

This offer is composed with a title, subtext, price and legal content.

Layout 4

This offer is composed with a title, a subtext, price, legal content and a tag.

Layout 5

This offer is composed with a title, a subtext, price, legal content and a bonus tag.

Prices

  • Price must be displayed consistently across all the pages.
  • Price should generally be in Bell blue font color. Only use. grey font as a secondary price, and white, when it's on a dark background.
  • Price should use Bell Slim Heavy
  • There are two different sizes for price: small and big.

Specs

Specs

Price with surtitle

Surtitle should be in Arial Regular 12px.

Price in-text

Price in-text should stay in Arial font, but should be highlighed in blue.
