Grid

  • Overview
  • The grid
  • How to treat dense content
  • How to use grid

Grids are composed of 12 columns. The grid is placed inside the container, having an outside margin of 15px each side. Between each column, we have a 30px margin (15px on each side of a column)

  • Normal gutter width of 30 px , with a possibility of 15px for dense content.
  • 12 columns on all views, mostly used on desktop.
  • 6 columns are used on tablet view. 4 columns used on mobile view.

How to treat dense content

When youre dealing with dense content, you can use this type of layout. The gutter can be 15 px in between elements. Generally use for layouts of 4 columns or more blocs of content.
